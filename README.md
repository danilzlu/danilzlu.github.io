# Danila Shubin

## Topology and Dynamical Systems

I am a researcher in dynamical systems and topology at HSE University.
My research interests include:

- Morse--Smale flows
- Dynamical systems on manifolds
- Topology of 3-manifolds
- Structural stability
- Topological classification of flows
